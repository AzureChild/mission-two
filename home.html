Yu-Gi-Oh was a classic show and card game that existed primarily in their childhood. For many it stopped there, however Yu-Gi-Oh has been continually updating and expanding on the franchise for over the last 20 years.

Though there have been plenty of products in recent times with Duel Links, Cross Duel, Speed Duels and Rush Duels, it was the release of Master Duel in 2022 that encouraged many fans, new and old, to return to this beloved childhood classic.

The Game Has Changed

However a lot has changed since the days of Duelist Kingdom. The complexity and speed of the game has gone well beyond the days of summoning La Jinn and setting Mirror Force. There is a common sentiment that the game is far too complex for new and returning players to understand.
